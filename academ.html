Features:

  • Dashboard allows you to quickly access all your priority subjects, and see how much is your Overall Score quickly.
  • Visualise your improvement with easy-to-read line graphs!
  • In the Subjects Tab, add your subjects with a button to get started. Just enter the weightage for your assessments and your final score and the rest will be done for you.
  • Haven't taken it yet? No problem! It'll even remind you that it's coming!
  • In Settings, change your marking scheme for PSLE, O/N Levels, JC, and Uni! We support GPA, MSG, AL, Overall Grade and MAG
  • Customize the app with themes to suit your tastes! Soft yellow, forest green, or your system settings!

With Academ, you can easily track your subjects' grades and set goals anytime, anywhere. Academ helps you stay focused on your goals throughout the year so you can always ace your assessments. Skip all the hassle with calculating your grades and upgrade your study game so you never miss your targets again.
